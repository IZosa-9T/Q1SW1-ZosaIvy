⌞ ✿ what is crocheting ? ✿ ⌝

Crocheting is the act of creating something out of yarn. Personally, I find it very fun and productive! You can create a variety of things using crochet.

Crocheting also serves as a stress reliever to clear your mind for a lot of people.

⌞ ✿ what can you crochet ? ? ✿ ⌝

  • you can make almost anything you want!
  • clothes (shirts, pants, dresses, etc)
  • bags to keep your personal belongings in.
  • stuffed toys for children or yourself

⌞ ✿ how do you crochet ? ? ✿ ⌝

  1. create a slipknot
  2. yarn over and pull yarn through to create one chain and repeat this step to create as many chains as you like
  3. insert hook into the 1st stitch
  4. yarn over
  5. pull yarn through
  6. yarn over again
  7. pull yarn through both loops
  8. chain one and repeat from step 3

⌞ ✿ what are the basic crochet stitches ? ? ✿ ⌝

  • double crocher (dc)
  • single crochet (sc)
  • slip stitch (ss)
  • half double crochet (hdc)
